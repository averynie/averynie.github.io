The Impact of The Don't Talk-a-Thon

When I was younger, my grandfather John Wetherhold passed from ALS, specifically bulbar ALS, which impaired his speech.

At eight years old, I took a vow of silence to honor him, raising money from friends and family. Catching the attention of the non-profit organization Project ALS, we created the Don't-Talk-a-Thon (DTAT).

Each year since then, during ALS awareness month, we ask others to take a vow of silence with us. Since the first DTAT, we have raised over $1M for ALS research.
